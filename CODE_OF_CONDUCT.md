## üéì Special Note for Beginners

**Your questions are welcome here.**

There's no such thing as a "stupid question" in this community. We all started somewhere, and asking questions is how we learn.

If someone makes you feel bad for asking a question, please report it.

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project team. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Project maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that don't align with this Code of Conduct.
